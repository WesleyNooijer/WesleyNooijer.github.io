This website represents a curated collection of new ideas in science and technology. The collection consists of ideas that I found most interesting and which I see as most significant for society.

Some of the major topics included on this website are: Biotechnology, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Nanotechnology, Blockchain, and Virtual Reality.

All ideas are collected in a public Roam Research graph. The major advantages of collecting ideas in Roam are the linking of ideas and the reusability of blocks of information. It is my hope that this will aid in the understanding of ideas and how they relate to other ideas.
